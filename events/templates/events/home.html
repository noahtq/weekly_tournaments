Welcome to

Weekly Tournaments

Mission Statement

Weekly Tournaments goal is to provide low commitment, fun tournaments with stat tracking and highlights available for each and every player. Weekly Tournaments was established in 2022 and is launching its first tournament this summer. The company is based in Minneapolis, Minnesota and primarly focuses on basketball tournaments but hopes to expand to other tournaments and events in the future.

Meet the owner

William Turnquist

Synopsis

William Turnquist is a 24-year old basketball enthusiast. Who fell in love with the game of basketball his sophomore year in highschool, ever since then he has played basketball as often as he can except for a one-year break because of a torn ACL. William says running Weekly Tournaments is his dream job.
"I hope to someday make this my career and have Weekly Tournaments location's all around the country that run multiple sports and maybe even other kinds of competitions every week." -William Turnquist
